About Me



Hi, I’m Meilia!

I am a UX/UI designer with a strong passion for crafting digital products that are both innovative and user-friendly.
Before starting my journey as a UX/UI designer, I have completed my degree in psychology and counselling.
Thus, my design process in grounded in empathy and understanding where I approach each of my projects with a user-centred mindset,
by considering the needs, goals, and behaviours of the target audience.

I am passionate about my work and strive to create designs that not only look great but also deliver a positive impact on users' lives.
My tools are Figma, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Fresco and Procreate.
When i’m not designing, I hang out with my dog, drink matcha latte, or work on my start up project.
